Match 8: dhoni's helicopter shots

watch highlights

The helicopter shot in Cricket is the act of flicking the bat toward the leg side.
when facing a yorker or a fuller-length delivery, and finishing the stroke with a flourish by twisting the bat in an overhead circle. This shot, which requires excellent timing and wrist-work, is considered a new innovation in cricket, and is seen as an unconventional form of batsmanship. Traditionally, faster bowlers have used yorker-length deliveries toward the end of limited-overs matches because it is difficult to hit such balls to the boundary; the helicopter shot is one answer to this tactic.
